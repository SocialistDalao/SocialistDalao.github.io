Keep open to the world

2021.01.07

Treat the world as the same,

though not.

Different people

with different names,

among whom I want to know

and not.

Talk to the world.

Listen to the world.

Might some time act like a fool

or thought to be good.

Might act same.

Might act different.

But that isn't what I should care.

Talk, and represent the true self to everyone.

Expression, feelings and thoughts.

What creates me is what is worth being given to the world.

Keep open, stay true.
